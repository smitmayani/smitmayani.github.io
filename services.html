Who we serve

Built for growing brands, ready for bigger operations.

Antivari is a strong fit when your brand has moved past casual self-fulfillment but does not want a faceless logistics partner.

Best fit

  • 100-1000 monthly orders, with room to scale beyond that
  • DTC-first brands using Shopify, WooCommerce, Shopware, or Amazon
  • Beauty, supplements, pet, eco-lifestyle, home goods, and premium products
  • Teams that care about speed, presentation, and inventory clarity

Less ideal

  • Fresh, frozen, or dangerous goods
  • Ultra-low-margin products where price is the only decision
  • Complex international customs as the main requirement
  • Brands with no repeatable order flow yet
